Plywood

Plywood is wood fabrication made of solid wood that is processed into sheets with the fiber direction on the wooden layer arranged transversely between the bottom layer and the surface layer using a special adhesive. In plywood production, a log is peeled into sheets of veneer which are then cut to the desired dimensions, dried, patched, glued together and then pressed under high temperature press to form the plywood panel. Depending on the type of log (quality, color etc.) peeled, the veneer sheets will become either face or back veneers, which are the furthest outer layers of the plywood panel, or core veneers which will form the inside layers.

Description

We make coconut shell charcoal briquettes for Shisha / hookah and for barbeque use. Our briquettes are made using only the highest quality coconut shell charcoal (which we produce ourselves), with a high carbon content and low ash content. The higher the fixed carbon, the higher the heat produced, the longer the briquette burns and the less ash is generated during combustion. Our coconut shell briquettes are hard and durable thanks to the high pressure used in production and the close monitoring of the drying process. Our briquettes are uniform and neat and can be made in various shapes and sizes. Strict quality control at all stages of production ensures consistently high quality. We can also pack briquettes according to your requirements.

Function

Charcoal briquettes are charcoal made from other types of charcoal which are mashed first and then printed as needed with a mixture of starch. The purpose of making charcoal briquettes is to increase the burn period and to save costs. Coconut shell charcoal can be used as charcoal briquettes, but only the crumbled shell. Meanwhile, an intact shell does not need to be turned into charcoal briquettes. Coconut shell charcoal briquettes are an alternative fuel that is often used for cooking, especially for baking foodstuffs and for shisha pipe smoking. Coconut shell charcoal briquettes produce more heat than coal briquettes. In addition, briquettes from coconut shell charcoal are also safer, environmentally friendly, because they do not cause smoke.
