WELCOME TO PARAMARTHA AGRO!



Paramartha Agro is a company engaged in the export trade of coconut briquettes charcoal and java preanger coffee. We are from Bandung City, Indonesia. We have exported to several countries in the Middle East.
For now, we usually make payments for orders via T/T which usually begins with a minimum Down Payment of 50%. We have not received payment via L/C for now.
We are used to shipping with the concept of FOB, CNF and CIF. for details you can contact us via WhatsApp or email.
We usually send via Tanjung Priouk Port - Jakarta, Tanjung Emas - Semarang and Tanjung Perak - Surabaya. All of them are located on the island of Java, Indonesia.
