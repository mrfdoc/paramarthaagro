Coconut Charcoal Briquettes

Indonesia is one of the largest coconut producers in the world with a very distinctive quality. The use of coconut, of course, besides being consumed as a food ingredient, it can also be made into charcoal briquettes.

The briquette charcoal produced from this coconut is extraordinary, has the perfect quality to be used as a substitute for fuel which of course has the advantage of a longer shelf life compared to ordinary charcoal. In addition, the use of coconut shell charcoal makes the briquettes have a fairly high burning heat and also less smoke, odorless and of course we have been certified.
